The Thinkubate Initiative

Starting May of 2017, Branch Out is proud to introduce the Thinkubate Initiative, a series of programs facilitating the incubation of creative ideas and entrepreneurial thinking. Thinkubate is part of our expansion into the Central Valley and focuses on democratizing the entrepreneurial education process by encouraging students to take ownership of their education, as well as that of generations of innovators to come.
