Our Programs

Introduction to Python

An introductory class to Python, one of the most widely used and powerful computer programming languages. Students will learn basic Python coding, including syntax, data types, control flow, lists, and Tkinter. By the end of the week, they'll have built their own video game similar to the popular Agar.io!
Ages 9-12.

Science of Design

In our Science of Design course, students will learn basic principles in aerodynamics and electricity/circuits as they build their own launchable plastic bottle rockets and tinker with household electronics.
