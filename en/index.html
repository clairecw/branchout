Our Philosophy

Intellectual curiosity and the enterprising spirit are elements that exist within all of us. Branch Out is devoted to unlocking them in students from all backgrounds. We firmly believe that by promoting entrepreneurial thinking skills within and outside the classroom, we can best prepare our nation’s future leaders to address a world of complex issues with 21st century critical thinking and problem solving skills.

Founders

Claire Wang and Shreya Parjan are both seniors in high school who have completed college-level coursework in computer science, business, math, science, engineering, government, and economics. They have collaborated on projects addressing issues ranging from youth unemployment to college readiness.

Shreya Parjan

Shreya Parjan is a social entrepreneur passionate about ending inequality of opportunity through the democratization of educational opportunities. She has conducted research on contemporary issues such as campaign finance reform and teacher housing in Silicon Valley. A mentor and STEAM program intern at Astra Women’s Business Alliance, Shreya is interested in program development and public policy and hopes to study physics, economics, and philosophy in college.

Claire Wang

Growing up in the Silicon Valley, Claire Wang was influenced by the wealth of STEAM opportunity in the area and adopted a strong passion for engineering. Proficient in a number of programming languages, she has tutored peers and helped teach beginner courses in computer science. Claire also enjoys learning language, Spanish in particular. She now works as a swim instructor and hopes to pursue a career in software development or computational linguistics.
