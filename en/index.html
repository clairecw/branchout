About

Branch Out is an organization that offers STEM and business courses free of charge. We currently teach physical sciences, coding, and business in both English and Spanish.

Our Philosophy

BranchOut is dedicated to educating young children. We firmly believe that education is the sole empowering factor to be able to carve out one's own future, and the most vital stage of this academic development is in the early years. All young students deserve this opportunity, regardless of background, and thus differentiate ourselves from the countless other STEAM and business camps in that we offer our programs free of cost. Thus, through these courses, we hope to better the learning environment of those from all backgrounds.

Founders

Claire and Shreya are incoming seniors at Monta Vista High School in Cupertino, California. Both interested in making a dent in the community, the two decided to dedicate themselves to BranchOut, bringing education to young students across diverse backgrounds.

Shreya Parjan

Shreya Parjan moved to the Silicon Valley from Portland, Oregon in 2014. She was taken aback by the vast amount of STEAM opportunities available to students and encouraged to start BranchOut with the hopes of making them accessible to all. In her free time, she enjoys pursuing interests in a vast variety of fields, ranging from biology to business to public policy and hopes to one day find a way to combine all of her interests into a viable career path.

Claire Wang

Growing up in the Silicon Valley, Claire Wang was influenced by the wealth of STEAM opportunity in the area and adopted a strong passion for engineering. Proficient in a number of programming languages, she has tutored peers and helped teach beginner courses in computer science. Claire also enjoys learning language, Spanish in particular. She now works as a swim instructor and hopes to pursue a career in software development or computational linguistics.
