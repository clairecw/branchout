#getdissatisfied Campus Campaign

Branch Out invites college and high school students across the US to join our #getdissatisfied campaign and network of student-led chapters. Why dissatisfaction? Well, we believe that anyone who wants to enact positive change in their communities ought to feel a little of it: something that just doesn’t feel right or acceptable in the status quo and pushes them to reach out, teach out, and Branch Out.

Goals for student chapters are as follows:
  • To encourage students to take ownership of their education
  • To give students a voice in local, state, and national public education
  • To cultivate student leaders who extend our goals of innovation in education across disciplines within the humanities, social sciences, and STEM
  • To serve underserved students and their families nationwide and develop meaningful partnerships with regional schools, nonprofits, governments, and businesses

If you’re ready to join our team and revolutionize public education in YOUR community, apply here!
