Our Programs

Innovation Lab

The summer 2016 Innovation Lab is comprised of 3 different courses in business, physical science, and engineering meshed into one week-long summer program.
We teach this program in both Spanish and English, although Spanish is a second language for us (so we'll be learning just as much as you!).

Introduction to Python

An introductory class to Python, one of the most widely used and powerful computer programming languages. Students will learn basic Python coding, including syntax, data types, control flow, lists, and Tkinter. By the end of the week, they'll have built their own video game similar to the popular Agar.io!

The Science of Design

In our Science of Design course, students will learn basic principles in aerodynamics and electricity/circuits as they build their own launchable plastic bottle rockets and tinker with household electronics.

Entrepreneurial Incubator

Students will create a consumer product from concept to design to marketing. At the week's end, they will pitch their product to a panel of judges and investors, Shark Tank-style.
