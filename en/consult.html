School Consulting

Our Model

Community-Tailored Action
Constantly shifting national public education standards can be difficult for educators to adapt to. We believe that each school district is an educational community with distinct strengths, needs, and challenges. Our curriculum consulting model recognizes that, offering lessons and activities to engage students in techniques to celebrate and improve their communities.

Design Thinking & Social Entrepreneurship
American public education has been in place for nearly 400 years. Yet in that time, little change has drastically affected the system. In the hopes of teaching students to translate their learning into innovative community action, Branch Out’s educational solutions are based on IDEO’s design thinking model.

Connections Across Dimensions
Despite the importance of scientific and technical programs in increasingly STEM-oriented educational and professional realms, we believe that the humanities and social sciences are essential to developing a student’s creative self.
